The Embassy San Francisco

The Embassy San Francisco is our experiment in creating a home built around purpose and intention. We value curiosity, analysis, questioning, and engagement. This is not a house for by-standers! If you like to jump in and be part of the fray, we think you'll like it here.

Density = Ideas +
Collaboration

A community of creatives and professionals sharing space in informal ways. Opportunities for organic and emergent discussions and collaborations. We're suckers for serendipity.

Action

We build community through doing stuff together! Think cooking, coworking, hosting but also reading clubs, art projects, skill shares, or sometimes just watching a movie. Events are held 2-3 nights per week.

Focus

We often work long hours. Our work may spill into home, but usually on purpose and with joy. We strive to do what we love. Daytimes at the house are more downbeat, and evenings often involve impromptu meals and wide ranging discussions.

Sharing, Openness, Exploration

We live in an age where the values and benefits of sharing and openness are exploding. We share food, space and ideas; we open our doors frequently; we often try new approaches to organizing ourselves.
